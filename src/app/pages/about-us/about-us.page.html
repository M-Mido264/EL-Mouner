About Us

ABOUT US :

At Almouneer, our vision is to effectively preserve the vision of diabetic patients through personalized quality care, state of the art technology and highly trained staff and our mission is to be the leader for all diabetic eye care services in the MENA region.

HISTORY & MISSION :

Almouneer was founded in response to the significant burden of diabetic vision loss in the region. Our co-founder Prof. Dr. Noha Khater, Egypt’s highly qualified eye surgeon and retinal disease specialist, aspired to protect diabetic patients’ eyes through offering specialized quality services. To bring her vision to life, Dr. Noha partnered up with Dr. Rania Kadry, IT expert, to create Egypt’s first specialized diabetic eye care clinics equipped with secure and efficient electronic health records.
